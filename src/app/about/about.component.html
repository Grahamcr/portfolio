THE SERIOUS STUFF

I am a Nelson Norman Group Certified UX professional with seven years of experience researching, managing, designing and developing experiences that make a difference. I view UX as a data driven design process, where every design decision is made using qualitative and quantitative evidence. I believe that the core of goal of UX should be to improve the lives of others whether it be making workflows more intuitive and efficient to use or creating software that keeps people safe.

The best part of UX is getting to work in a collaborative environment where your goal everyday is to research and create solutions to complex challenges.
