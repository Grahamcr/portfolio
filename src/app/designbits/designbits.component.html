The Change the Narrative Project

This is a personal passion project of mine that I completed in the fall of 2019. My wife and I were foster parents who eventually were blessed with the opportunity to adopt our foster child after he was in our care for over three years. The experience was life changing and eye opening. I realized how my misconceptions and stereotypes about foster care exist in the world, many of which I believed myself before coming a foster parent. I decided to try to do something to change that and started the Change the Narrative Project.
