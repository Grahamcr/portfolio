Access to data the way customers think

Users come to Command Center with a goal in mind, often one that is time sensitive. Therefore I took care to make sure information was organized specifically to match their mental model.

Beautiful and more importantly, functional

While it was important that Command Center was aesthetically pleasing to help with sales, it was even more important that what was being display was actionable for the user.

Quick access to related information

To support our user's goal of getting to actionable information fast I made sure supporting information was always easily accessible, no matter how they entered the experience.

Leveraging consistency

Every details page follows the same design pattern of putting high level, important and frequently accessed information at the top, followed by the supporting information.
