Building a Internal UX Brand

As part of a very early beta version of the design system I created while at vMOX I started a blog called What Has Design Done Lately?. While I am not proud of the design, I am proud of the impact the blog had. Each post had three parts, what has design done lately, what is design planning to do and a new or interesting UX fact.

Tone Matters

I intentionally wrote each post in a very conversational tone with hopes of driving adoption and retaining my coworkers interest. While I covered very important topics, I also took a bit of time to mix in fun Easter eggs to encourage the reader to take the time to at least scan the entire content of the post.

Seeing is Believing

If you are interested to find out more and see of the stories I shared with our technology team please visit the blog at Kiwi Beta Blog

The Start of Something More

This blog and the rest of the original Kiwi Design Site was a beta to test out how the team might respond to the development of a proper design system. The success of the beta site led me to create a project proposal which became one of the four company wide goals for vMOX/Advantage in 2019. The resulting design system has saved hundreds of hours of development and design effort. If you would like to find out more check out the Kiwi Design System Design Bit.
